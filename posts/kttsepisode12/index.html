About Me

In the past, Topher has worked as a TV host, a casting director’s associate for Paramount Pictures, a musician, a radio DJ, and a director. Topher is now an executive assistant at NBCUniversal. He also freelances on the side as a comedian, host, and writer in Los Angeles. He studies at the Upright Citizens Brigade, writes/ hosts a live show in LA called, “It’s Past My Bedtime with Topher Harless,” and hosts the podcasts Kristen Teaches Topher Sports and Wine and Comics.
