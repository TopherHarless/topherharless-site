About Me

In the past, Topher has worked as a TV host, a casting director’s associate for Paramount Pictures, an executive assistant at NBCUniversal, a musician, a radio DJ, and a director. Topher is now a Creative Services Coordinator for International Marketing at NBCUniversal. He also freelances on the side as a massive comic book nerd, comedian, podcast host, and writer in Los Angeles. He studies at the Upright Citizens Brigade and hosts the podcast Wine and Comics.
