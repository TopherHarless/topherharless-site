Wine and Comics Episode 30: Flash Rebirth – Part Two

Download on iTunes: https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/wineandcomicss-podcast/id992335068

In my last podcast before I leave for France, Meghan and I recorded episode thirty of Wine and Comics.  In this episode, we finish reading the graphic novel, Flash Rebirth.

It’s a DC Comics story by Geoff Johns (Author), Ethan Van Sciver (Illustrator).

If you’re watching the TV show The Flash on the CW, this episdoe and the last might fill in some fan theories.  It was really fin to read, but a bit hard to explain, so I suggest you pick up a copy for yourself.
